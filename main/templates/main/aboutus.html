About Us

Our Mission

We want to open the doors for job seekers everywhere to the great employment opportunities at the City of Los Angeles, Making it easier and clearer how to apply.

Our Team

Work for LA is a project born in the Hack for LA community and built by volunteers from a wide range of professional industries, coming together from different backgrounds with one objective: creating a platform to help people understand and navigate the overcomplicated hiring process for Civil Service jobs with the City of Los Angeles.

Hack for LA

Hack for LA is the official Los Angeles chapter of Code for America, a national nonprofit that believes government can work for the people, by the people, in the 21st century. We bring together coders, designers, entrepreneurs, students, government staff, activists, and other civi-minded people to solve the LA region’s biggest civic challenges.

Want to Contribute? Join Us!

Hack for LA hosts Civic Hack Night weekly on Tuesday nights at 6:00 p.m. Located in the Los Angeles Arts District at The Los Angeles Cleantech Incubator (LACI), La Kretz Innovation Campus.
