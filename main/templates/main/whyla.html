Competitive Pay

The City offers competitive pay and yearly raises that cover the cost of living.

Excellent Benefits

As a City employee, you receive excellent benefits that include a pension and health insurance.

Generous Personal Time Off

As a City employee, you receive yearly vacation, sick days and every other Friday off.

Meaningful Work

After you receive your score, you’ll begin receiving job notification emails. (The higher your score, the sooner you’ll start receiving them.) Call the number on the notification email to tell them that you’re interested in interviewing. (The Department will interview the candidates with the top scores who call in, so if you don’t get scheduled for an interview, don’t worry, and just call in for the next one.)
